Understanding Depression

What is Depression?

Depression is more than just feeling sad. It's a serious mental health condition that affects how you feel, think, and handle daily activities.

Common Symptoms

  • Persistent sad, anxious, or "empty" mood
  • Feelings of hopelessness or pessimism
  • Loss of interest in hobbies and activities
  • Decreased energy or fatigue
  • Difficulty sleeping or oversleeping
  • Changes in appetite or weight

Treatment Options

  • Psychotherapy (Talk Therapy)
  • Medication (when prescribed by a healthcare provider)
  • Lifestyle changes
  • Support groups
  • Mindfulness and meditation

Coping Strategies

  • Maintain a regular daily routine
  • Exercise regularly
  • Stay connected with friends and family
  • Practice stress-reduction techniques
  • Get adequate sleep

Need Professional Help?

If you're struggling with depression, remember that help is available. Speak with a mental health professional or contact our crisis hotline.
