Understanding Anxiety

What is Anxiety?

Anxiety is your body's natural response to stress. While some anxiety is normal, excessive worry and fear that interfere with daily life may indicate an anxiety disorder.

Common Symptoms

  • Excessive worrying
  • Restlessness or feeling on edge
  • Rapid heartbeat or breathing
  • Difficulty concentrating
  • Sleep problems
  • Physical symptoms like sweating or trembling

Treatment Options

  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
  • Anti-anxiety medications (when prescribed)
  • Relaxation techniques
  • Support groups
  • Lifestyle modifications

Coping Strategies

  • Practice deep breathing exercises
  • Use grounding techniques
  • Regular exercise
  • Limit caffeine and alcohol
  • Maintain a consistent sleep schedule

Need Professional Help?

If anxiety is affecting your daily life, professional help is available. Reach out to a mental health professional or contact our crisis hotline.
