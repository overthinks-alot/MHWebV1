Self-Care Practices

What is Self-Care?

Self-care means taking time to pay attention to yourself, both physically and mentally. It's about making your health and well-being a priority.

Physical Self-Care

  • Regular exercise and movement
  • Healthy eating habits
  • Getting enough sleep
  • Regular medical check-ups
  • Taking breaks when needed

Mental Self-Care

  • Practicing mindfulness
  • Journaling
  • Setting healthy boundaries
  • Engaging in hobbies
  • Seeking therapy when needed

Daily Self-Care Ideas

  • Take a relaxing bath or shower
  • Practice deep breathing exercises
  • Spend time in nature
  • Connect with loved ones
  • Read a book or listen to music

Need Support?

Remember, seeking help is a form of self-care. If you're struggling, reach out to our support team or mental health professionals.
