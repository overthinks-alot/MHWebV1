Managing Stress

Understanding Stress

Stress is your body's response to pressure. While some stress can be motivating, chronic stress can negatively impact your physical and mental health.

Signs of Stress

  • Feeling overwhelmed or frustrated
  • Difficulty relaxing
  • Changes in eating or sleeping habits
  • Physical symptoms like headaches or muscle tension
  • Difficulty concentrating
  • Increased irritability

Stress Management Techniques

  • Mindfulness and meditation
  • Regular exercise
  • Time management strategies
  • Setting boundaries
  • Progressive muscle relaxation

Lifestyle Changes

  • Maintain a balanced diet
  • Get adequate sleep
  • Practice regular self-care
  • Take regular breaks
  • Connect with supportive people

Need Additional Support?

If stress is overwhelming you, professional help is available. Connect with a stress management specialist or counselor.
